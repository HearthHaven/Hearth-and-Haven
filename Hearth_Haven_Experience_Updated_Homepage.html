Woman- and Veteran-Owned

Hearth & Haven Comforts LLC is proudly owned and operated by a woman and veteran. With a background in military service, I learned the importance of dedication, discipline, and attention to detail. These values are at the heart of everything we do.

As a corporate professional and frequent traveler, I’ve experienced firsthand what it’s like to stay in accommodations that lack the essentials for comfort and convenience. From forgetting basic toiletries to missing the feeling of being at home, I realized how much of a difference thoughtful details can make.

That’s why Hearth & Haven was created — to provide warm, welcoming spaces equipped with everything you need to feel truly at ease during your stay. Whether it’s a fully stocked kitchen, cozy furnishings, or the little touches that make all the difference, our goal is to ensure you feel cared for, no matter where your journey takes you.

What Makes Hearth & Haven Special

At Hearth & Haven, our name is more than just words—it reflects our commitment to creating spaces that bring warmth, comfort, and security to every guest.

The Meaning Behind Our Name:

  • Hearth: The hearth is the heart of a home, where warmth and care bring people together. It symbolizes coziness, hospitality, and the feeling of being nurtured. At Hearth & Haven, we embrace this by crafting spaces that invite you to relax, recharge, and feel cared for—whether you’re traveling for work or pleasure.
  • Haven: A haven is a safe and peaceful refuge, a place where you can escape the chaos of daily life. We create properties designed to be calm, inviting sanctuaries, offering all the comforts you need to feel truly at ease during your stay.
